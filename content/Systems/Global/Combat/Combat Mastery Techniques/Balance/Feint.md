>[!warning] Alert!
>Feints do not make ‘harmful’ contact.
# Level 1

- Gives -1 RAC & Block SPD towards the following strike.
- 1 turn cooldown.
- Consumes 0 attacks.
# Level 2

 - Gives -1 RAC & -2 Block SPD towards the following strike.
 - 1 turn cooldown.
 - Consumes 0 attacks.

# Level 3

- Gives -2 RAC & -3 Block SPD towards the following strike.
- 2 turn cooldown.
- Consumes 0 attacks.
